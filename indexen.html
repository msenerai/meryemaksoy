The Method

What is QHHT?

Quantum Healing Hypnosis Technique is a subconscious regression therapy developed by Dolores Cannon over 50+ years of research.

QHHT uses deep hypnosis to reach the subconscious — the Theta wave state — enabling exploration of past lives, understanding the roots of illness and obstacles, and establishing direct communication with the Higher Self. The aim is not to judge, but to understand and heal.
